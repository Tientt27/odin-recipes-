FRENCH FRIES WITH FRIED CHICKEN

Classic french fries with fried chicken recipe

They are tasty and unhealthy but lots of people love them.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • to taste Salt

COOKING STEPS:

  1. Wash the potatoes. Peel them.

  2. Then divide it in to small little strips.

  3. Heat oil for frying. Then fry the fries.
